Founder note

I started Linden Assurance around a simple idea: most teams can launch an AI feature, but very few can prove that it behaves safely, consistently, and truthfully under pressure. That gap is where liability lives, and it is where Linden intends to work.

Why This Company

A mission built around trust, verification, and real-world consequences

Independent mindset

The vision is to act as an external assurance layer, not a model vendor marking its own homework.

High-stakes focus

Legal, healthcare, and financial systems deserve deeper verification because mistakes in those environments can trigger compliance, litigation, and human harm.

Long-term ambition

Linden is being positioned toward a larger trust standard, where historical failure data, audit evidence, and structured scoring can support enterprise and underwriting decisions.

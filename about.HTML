About Us

Trust is not a vibe. It is a standard, a process, and an audit trail.

Linden Assurance is being shaped around one core belief: high-stakes AI should be tested like infrastructure, not marketed like magic.

What we are building

A client-facing assurance platform that combines adversarial pressure testing, deterministic validation, and structured reporting. The long-term goal is to help organizations understand whether an AI system is reliable enough for real deployment in regulated or liability-heavy contexts.

What we are not building

Linden is not another general-purpose chatbot. The product direction is verification, auditing, and trust scoring for AI systems already being used by organizations.

Method

Our proposed operating model

1. Scope and define failure

Before testing begins, the system under review needs a clear domain, approved use case, structured outputs, and a strict definition of what counts as failure.

2. Pressure-test with adversarial scenarios

Audit plans can include fact checks, contradiction cascades, prompt-injection attempts, reasoning traps, and domain-specific edge cases tailored to healthcare, legal, or finance.

3. Verify against trusted records

The strongest version of Linden uses grounded evaluation and deterministic checks so that outputs are measured against evidence, not persuasive wording alone.

4. Return a decision-ready report

The final output is intended to include a determination, a failure taxonomy, a risk score, and a remediation path where applicable.

Illustrative Ratings

The Linden badge system can become a clear public trust signal

Linden Certified

Target tier for highly reliable systems with minimal failures in scope.

Linden Verified

Conditional or lower-risk pass state with disclosed limits and recommended fixes.

Linden Fail

Threshold not met. System requires remediation before trust signaling is justified.
